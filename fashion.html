Background Resumé Projects Connect

The goal of my intern project at TJX is to marry the business needs of driving e-commerce innovation and the technical needs of the company by supporting them in their transition to Microsoft Azure. Therefore, my team presented an artificially intelligent outfit recommendation system that caters to a customer’s style while utilizing TJMaxx’s cycling inventory. Our final system consists of a Python backend powered by the Microsoft Azure’s Image Recognition API that trains a machine learning model to recognize patterns, colors, and other attributes of clothing and finds apparel on TJMaxx.com with similar qualities. Additionally, we presented a front-end website that sits on top of the backend and provides a possible integration of the prediction service within the e-commerce site. My role was developing the Front-End website and managing the API connections.
