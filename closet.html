Background Resumé Projects Connect

Choose My Clothing!, is a virtual closet application meant to suggest clothing based on weather conditions and connect people through their clothes. The first ever social media based on clothing choices, we want people to interact with their friend’s closets and bond through their love of fashion. Other competing apps mainly target women and are limited to using only the weather or social occasion as clothing classifiers, while our app is meant to be inclusive and help those of all ages and genders who have trouble deciding what to wear. Users can view and comment on their friend’s outfits, see what clothing is compatible with what weather type, and generate outfits. This project was built using Django, HTML, Bootstrap and custom CSS.
